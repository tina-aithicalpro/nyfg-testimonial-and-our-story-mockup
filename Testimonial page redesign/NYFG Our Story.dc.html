Our Story

Who We Are

New York Flower Group is a wholesale flower importer and distributor based in the heart of the New York City Wholesale Flower Market. We carry over 25,000 SKUs and serve roughly 6,000 clients nationwide, sourcing from international growers across Holland, Japan, New Zealand, France, South America, and Asia, alongside local organic farms close to home.

Where We Came From

New York Flower Group came together in 2020, bringing three established NYC flower companies under one name: Dutch Flower Line, G. Page Wholesale Flowers, and A Rose By Harvest. Each had built its own reputation in the wholesale flower market over decades. Combining them let us carry a deeper, more diverse inventory and a stronger network of growers than any one company could offer on its own.

Where We Are

Today, New York Flower Group operates three locations in New York City:

  • NYFG #120 — 120 West 28th Street, New York, NY 10001
  • NYFG #150 — 150 West 28th Street, New York, NY 10001
  • NYFG Long Island City — 21-43 44th Road, Long Island City, NY 11101, our newest location at 30,000 square feet

Where We're Going
